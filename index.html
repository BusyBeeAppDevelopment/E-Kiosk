NEW FRANCHISEE REQUIREMENTS:

Scales ‘N Tails net worth and capital requirements are:

  • Minimum of $50,000 in cash or liquid assets
  • Minimum net worth of $150,000
  • Ability of obtain financing up to $250,000

These are minimum requirements and do not represent the total potential costs to open and operate one or more Scales ‘N Tails units.

SCALES ‘N TAILS FRANCHISEE QUALIFICATIONS ARE:

  • Business Professionals with Management Experience
  • Retail Experience Is Preferred
  • Animal Experience Is Preferred
